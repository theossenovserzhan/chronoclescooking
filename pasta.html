Step by step cooking classic pasta

  1. 1. First of all, you should boil the pasta in salted water. They should turn out a little undercooked.
  2. 2. Drain 0.5 tablespoons of broth, it will be useful in the future when preparing pasta, and then the spaghetti must be thrown into a colander to drain excess liquid. Pour olive oil into a frying pan, add chili and warm it up.
  3. 3. After removing the skin from the chicken thighs and separating the meat from the bones, cut it into pieces and send it to the hot pepper.
  4. 4. We settle the chicken according to the pasta recipe and put it on a plate.
  5. 5. Next, peel the garlic from the husk, chop it with a knife and send it to the frying pan.
  6. 6. We cut the washed cherry tomatoes into halves and add them to the garlic.
  7. 7. Pour the contents of the frying pan with the left broth from the pasta and put it out a little.
  8. 8. At the next stage of cooking pasta at home, add chicken and pasta to the vegetables.
  9. 9. After mixing the ingredients, salt and pepper, sprinkle with cheese crushed on a coarse grater.
  10. 10. Chop the parsley and decorate the pasta with herbs.
