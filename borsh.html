Step by step cooking borsh

  1. 1. Pour water into a saucepan, put the meat and turn on medium heat. Cook for about an hour.
  2. 2. Wash and peel the beets, carrots and onions. Grate the beets on a coarse grater and sprinkle with lemon juice. Thanks to this, the borscht will be really red. Grate the carrots on a medium grater. Cut the onion into cubes.
  3. 3. Pour sunflower oil into a frying pan, turn on medium heat. First fry the onion and carrot (5 minutes), then add the beetroot.
  4. 4. Fry the vegetables for another 5 minutes. After that, add the tomato paste, stir and leave on the fire for another 5-7 minutes.
  5. 5. When the broth is cooked, remove the meat from it. While the meat is cooling, lower the chopped cabbage into the broth. After 5-10 minutes, add the potatoes cut into strips.
  6. 6. While the potatoes are cooking, separate the meat from the bone and cut into cubes. Return the meat to the soup. Add the roast and stir.
  7. 7. Add the bay leaf and finely chopped herbs. Cover the pan with a lid and cook for another 5-7 minutes.
  9. 8. Serve the borscht hot with a spoonful of sour cream.
